ABOUT ME.

Haixiang Kevin Peng is a photographer, cinematographer, and wrestler based in Ann Arbor. Born and raised in Shanghai, China in 2002, Kevin is now a junior student in Umich, double majoring in Business Economics and School of Information and will graduate in June 2025.

Driven by a deep passion for photography and creating cinematic content, Kevin is committed to integrating his experience in marketing, finance, and the business landscape with his love for art, particularly photography and cinematography.

His goal is to work on cutting-edge entertainment projects that leverage new technologies in the realms of entertainment management or film production. Thus, he is eager to explore various opportunities to connect with like-minded professionals who’s also dedicated to the film and entertainment industry.
